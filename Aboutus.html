About our shop

  • Electric Tattoo was first established in 2018 by Ryan Ashely Malarkey and is located in LA California. 7274 Melrose Ave, Los Angeles, CA 90046
  • Ryan Ashley was the season 8 winner of Ink Master and decided it was time to open up her own shop.
  • The idea behind the shop was to create a more feminine atmosphere that makes female artists and customers feel more comfortable.
  • Since opening, it has become a five star tattoo shop, hiring the most talented artists from all of the country.
  • We have approximatley 7 artists who each reserve their own stations.
  • There is also a sister shop opening in Miami Florida this December.
  • To reach the shop for any questions, please call 323-545-7956.
